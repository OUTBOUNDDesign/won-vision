Mobile services — variants

Three mobile-first directions for the Services section. Each frame shows roughly what a 360–420px-wide phone would render. Sora 500 for the headings, 400 for body.

M1 — Stacked photo tilesRecommended
Five vertical full-width cards. Photo + service name as Archivo Black caption bottom-left + a small "view" cue. One tap navigates straight to /book#cat-…. Photos do the storytelling on a screen where there's no hover. Closest cousin to AC Media's tiles, but stacked.
