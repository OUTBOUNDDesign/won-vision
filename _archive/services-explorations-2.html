V7 — Numeral-led rows

Huge faded numeral behind each row

Every row has a massive Archivo Black numeral (01, 02 …) ghosted in the background at 6% opacity. The numbers do the design work; the photo can stay small and disciplined. Strong, typographic, very studio-monograph.
