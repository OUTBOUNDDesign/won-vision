Brief · 2026

Ten hero directions and twelve slogan candidates for Won Media — pick the lines and frames that feel like a Melbourne property practice, not a photographer.

All variants use the locked palette (Paper, Soft Grey, Steel, Graphite, Ink) and locked typography (Abhaya Libre 500 + Italic 400, Inter for utility). No radial pattern. AI photo placeholders are pulled from Picsum and will be replaced with real Melbourne photography on launch.

Scroll through. Each frame is labelled. Pick a hero (single letter or a mix), and circle the slogan you want to lead with — including any rewrites you'd make.

Slogan candidates.

The current locked tagline is Property, considered. — kept here as the baseline. The rest are written within the brief's voice rules: editorial, quiet, specific, no superlatives. Italics indicate the line reads better in Abhaya Italic; upright lines work in Abhaya Medium.
